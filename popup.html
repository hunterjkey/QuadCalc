QuadCalc!

It's known that having to solve any problem related to the quadratic equation can be a big pain. QuadCalc is a simple, but effective chrome extension designed to eliminate this pain. You can solve any real quadratic with this here extension. Even, if your math teacher is one of the "give me your answer in simplified radical form" teachers, we've got you covered there too. Just give it a click and watch it work its magic!
